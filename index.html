Privacy Policy for ScoreSync

Last updated: February 2026

ScoreSync is a score tracking application for Wear OS devices.

Information We Collect

  • Game scores and room codes
  • Anonymous device identifiers
  • Crash reports (device model and OS version))

How We Use Information

  • Provide multiplayer room functionality
  • Improve app stability through crash analysis

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your information. Data is stored securely.
